Facet 4 · the catch

Two failure modes to plan for

Stampede on cold keys

A popular key expiring sends every request to Postgres at once. Mitigate with a short jittered lock or request coalescing.

Stale after a write

Write-around means the cache keeps the old value until TTL. Reads can lag a write by up to the TTL window.
